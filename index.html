What is good design?

Most of my familiarity with design comes from the space of urban design and architecture and less from the realm of graphic design. Good design is something that is both visually stimulating and useful. There are many instances where things can look pretty but don't function well, and so I think good design marries the two ideas. In terms of graphic design, I know that these two ideas, visual interest and function, are just as important- it just manifests itself in different ways.
